Project Experience

My first project was designing and developing a social media wall for 4ORE! Golf The social wall pulls hashtags and displays posts in a grid allowing guests to showcase their experiences. Posts are flipped and rotated every five seconds. I created two versions of the social wall; one is used in the main lobby and the other is used on the company website.


For my senior capstone project I worked alongside a group of software developers to create an unbiased news outlet using the Django web framework in Python. My role was to set up the database to store articles, display articles in an appealing way, and implement a save and share feature for end users. We decided to present each article in a modal (enlarged view) so users are able to focus on one article at a time.

We developed two algorithms that we believed separated our site from other news outlets, which were basic fact checking and sentiment analysis. The fact checking algorithm used websites such as Snopes and factChecker to check the accuracy of every article. Sentiment analysis determines the attitude of the article text and data is presented in the following categories: positive, negative, and neutral. The results were displayed in a pie chart. While we were able to successfully showcase our project's concept, we believe it can be improved.
